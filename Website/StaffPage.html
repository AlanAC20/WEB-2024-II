THE BEST "JOB" YOU WILL EVER HAVE

Climb a mountain, canoe down a river, enjoy a round of golf, take a tennis lessons, learn how to play the guitar and relax at the beach. Here is our promise: the skills learned and memories made at Camp Wildwood will last a lifetime.

EMPLOYMENT OPPORTUNITIES

Camp wildwood's staff must be at least 18 years old, posotive, and love kids! No formal professional experience is necessary, just a willingness to immerse yourself in the experience. 85% of our 200+ staff members have already completed at least two years of college. Camp can be a fluid and fast paced environment, and we look for creative and flexible people who can be counted on as "team players".

TEAM SPORTS COACHING JOBS AT WILDWOOD

At Camp Wildwood, team sports are an essential component of the daily schedule. We aim to hire aspiring coaches who are not only looking to positively impact the campers, but also who are hoping to cultivate their own teaching skills. Our Wildwood Coaching Guide lays the framework for how team sports are taught at camp. We will help train you as you train the boys.
